Rules of Bingo

Back
  • Use your NetID or non-Notre Dame email address to get your BINGO card
  • Throughout the day, we will call the bingo numbers by SMS and Twitter. Follow @NDMobileSummit for the numbers. Numbers will be called during breaks and transitions between speakers.
  • To win, you must match five numbers (with or without the free space) in a row or diagonal across the board
  • If you have a Bingo, An Alert will appear giving you your choice on how to contact us to verify (twitter, Text-message, ect.)
  • Please do not interrupt the conference by shouting out Bingo
  • We will play until all prizes have been awarded. Some prizes may be taxable. Winners must be attendees of the conference and must be present to claim their prizes
  • The game will take place only during the Notre Dame Mobile Summit on September 28, 2012.
