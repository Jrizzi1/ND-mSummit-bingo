Rules

  • Use your NetID or non-Notre Dame email address to get your BINGO card
  • Throughout the day, we will Tweet the BINGO numbers. Follow @NDMobileSummit for the numbers or text "FOLLOW ndmobilesummit" to 40404 to receive Twitter alerts by SMS. Numbers will be called during breaks and transitions between speakers.
  • To win, you must match five numbers (with or without the free space) in a row or diagonal across the board
  • If you have a B-I-N-G-O, a popup will appear giving you your choice on how to contact us to verify. In case this popup does not appear, tweet @NDMobileSummit or send a text message to (574) 344-1987 with BINGO and the Email / user ID you used to generate your bingo card with.
  • Please do not interrupt the conference by shouting out BINGO!
  • We will play until all prizes have been awarded. Some prizes may be taxable. Winners must be attendees of the conference and must be present to claim their prizes
  • The game will take place only during the Notre Dame Mobile Summit on October 11, 2013.
